WHO ARE WE?

Treynex is a Kenyan aerospace manufacturer and suborbital spaceflight company. We focus on developing and launching rockets. Treynex Aerospace's main mission is to design, develop, and manufacture high-performance launch vehicles and satellite systems that are reliable and cost-effective.

OUR MISSION

Treynex Aerospace is set to commit itself to revolutionizing space exploration by leveraging Kenya’s strategic equatorial location to develop and launch cost-effective, reusable rockets. It will aim at building and developing advanced satellites that contribute to monitoring and collecting useful information that will help in solving critical challenges in Africa, from agriculture to climate change and communication. By utilizing locally sourced materials and innovative, affordable production methods, we are dedicated to drastically reduce the costs of space missions, making space technology accessible and of benefit to Africa's development and sustainability.

OUR VISION

Our vision at Treynex Aerospace is to lead the continent into a new space age, where it will become a central player in the global space exploration, making it grab a share of the market. We envision a future where Kenya's strategic location at the equatorial region is fully leveraged for the most efficient and cost-effective satellite launches, making the country a hub for space activities on the continent. We strive to innovate in launch vehicle reusability, cutting down the the costs of space missions, and to contribute to Africa's growth by using space technology to solve local problems, driving economic development, and position Africa as a key player in the global space industry.
