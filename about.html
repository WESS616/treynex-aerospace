ABOUT US

Who We Are

Treynex is a Kenyan aerospace manufacturer and suborbital spaceflight company. The company will focus on both developing and launching useful satellites into orbit for customers and for the company itself for offering related services. Treynex Aerospace's main mission is to design, develop, and manufacture high-performance launch vehicles that are highly reliable and relatively cheaper to launch, making space more accessible.

Our approach will combine deep research and development and use of the latest technologies with practical engineering solutions, all of these with a target of making space access more affordable and sustainable for stakeholders and new ventures across the continent and to the rest of the world.

Treynex Aerospace was founded in 2018 by James Mwaura with the vision of revolutionizing space exploration and showcasing Africa, particularly Kenya, as a valuable participant in the global space industry. At a young age, James immersed himself in the field, driven by a passion for innovation and a determination to prepare for a future where Treynex would contribute meaningfully to humanity’s journey into space. Despite limited resources, he spent years visualizing and planning for the company’s success, ensuring it would be ready when the right moment arrived.

As both the Chief Executive Officer (CEO) and Chief Technology Officer (CTO), James Mwaura will lead Treynex through its foundational stages, providing strategic and technical guidance to establish a strong foundation for the company. His leadership reflects a deep commitment to fostering technological advancements and positioning Kenya and Africa as active participants in shaping the future of space exploration.

James plans to transition from his roles as CEO and CTO once the company is operational and well-positioned for growth. This transition will enable Treynex to attract specialized leadership and expertise to further its mission while ensuring the vision and principles established during its founding remain central. Through Treynex, James aims to inspire the next generation of African innovators and demonstrate the continent’s capacity to actively participate in the global space exploration landscape.
